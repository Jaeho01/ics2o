Requirements for my Video Game

  • On the canvas, there will be a screen with the instructions and at the bottom there will be a start button
  • When you press the start button, it will change the canvas to the game screen
  • On the game screen, there will be a ball, a rectangle at the bottom to begin with
  • The ball will start bouncing off the left, right, top wall, and the rectangle at the bottom when the game starts
  • You can control the rectangle at the bottom with the arrow keys. The rectangle will only move sideways
  • The speed of the ball will slowly increase each time when it hits the rectangle
  • The goal is to get the highest score and beat my score
  • Extra ball will be put into the game when the score is 7
  • If you want more challenge, press c and the speed of the brick will decrease
