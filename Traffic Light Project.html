Traffic Light Project

Basic Requirements For Two Coordinated Sets Of Red, Yellow, Green Lights

  • Green light stays on for 35 seconds
  • Green light takes 1 second to turn yellow
  • Yellow light stays on for 3 seconds
  • Yellow light takes 1 second to turn red
  • It takes 1 second for the other traffic light to turn green when the first traffic light is red
  • Repeat
  • The two traffic lights can not be the same colour because if they are they will be very dangerous. More explanation on the next page.
