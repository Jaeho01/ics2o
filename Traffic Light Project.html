Traffic Light Project

Basic Requirements For Two Coordinated Sets Of Red, Yellow, Green Lights

  • Green light stays on for 35 seconds
  • 1 seconds for the green light to turn yellow
  • Yellow light stays on for 3 seconds
  • 1 second for the yellow light to turn red
  • 1 second from one traffic light turns red to the the other one turning green
  • While one traffic light is green, the other one is red. They never can be the same or it will be dangerous
