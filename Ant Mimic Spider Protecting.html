How to Protect Your Computer from Spam

Image from Dangerous Common Sense

Since you learned what spam can do, you will want to know how to protect your computer and you. One of the best ways is using a spam filter. A spam filter is a program that is used to detect unsolicited and unwanted email and prevent those messages from getting to a user's inbox. By having this program on your computer, it will prevent most of the spam e-mails from going to your inbox. These spams will be in spam section in your e-mail. Another good thing about this program is that your e-mail account usually has some kind of spam filter. That's why you see 'Spam' in one of the catagories. When you receive a spam mail, the spam filter that is already built in will filter that to the spam section in your e-mail.
