Title

Traffic light is an important tool in our lives. According to Google “Traffic light is a set of automatically operated colored lights, typically red, amber, and green, for controlling traffic at road junctions and crosswalks”. This tool in our lives can be very simple but very complicated in some ways. Most people know the basic rules: red means stop, yellow means slow down, and green means go. This have kept us safe on the roads for many years but more rules were made in order to keep us even more safe. There are different traffic light rules in different provinces and different countries. Even the shape of traffic light may look different based on the province and the country.

Like I said above, there are many traffic light rules. The basic rule is to stop when red light is on, slow down when yellow light is on and go when green light is on. Turning left is a little bit different than going forward. The traffic light will look like this image on the left.

Like the previous picture when both green light and the green arrow is on, that means the driver may go forward, turn left, or turn right in the proper lane. When the driver sees a traffic light like this image on the right,

There is another signals that are in our lives that also helped us a lot and that is pedestrian-signals. Pedestrian-signals have the same purpose with the traffic lights but for pedestrian. These signals looks like one of these pictures on the right
but sometimes with a timer at the bottom. Pedestrian-signals are very simple, red means stop, white means go, and flashing white means that the light will turn red soon. The signal with a timer will show how many seconds are left until the light turns red.
