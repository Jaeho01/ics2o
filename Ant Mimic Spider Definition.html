Definition of Spam

Image from Google Play Store

Like I said on the introductions page, I will be providing informations about spam e-mails and spam messages. There are different types of spam. Before I go on to details let's look at the definition of Spam. Spam is irrelevant or inappropriate messages sent on the Internet to a large number of recipients. People usually think that only way to receive spam is e-mail but it isn't. It can be sent in many different ways including Facebook Messenger, Instagram and even Snapchat but I will mostly talk about e-mail spams.
