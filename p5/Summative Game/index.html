Requirements for my Video Game

  • The goal is to destroy all the bricks on the screen
  • You will have one ball to start
  • When the ball touches the bottom the game's over
  • The ball wil bounce off the left, right, and the top wall.
  • You have the control to a brick at the bottom
  • That brick can move side ways and it will bounce off the balls
  • The speed will increase everytime it bounces off the walla and the brick at the bottom
  • After all the bricks are destroyed, you win!!
